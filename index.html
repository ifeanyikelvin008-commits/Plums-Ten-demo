Our Expert Services

Our expert plumbing services are designed to support residential and commercial properties with reliable, code-compliant solutions. Our licensed plumbers handle installation, repairs, and maintenance of plumbing systems, ensuring proper water flow, drainage, and long-term performance.

Leak Detection & Repairs

We locate and repair hidden and visible leaks to prevent water damage, mold growth, and high utility bills.

Fixture Installation & Replacement

Professional installation and replacement of faucets, sinks, toilets, showers, and valves to ensure proper operation and efficiency.

Drain Cleaning & Clog Removal

Clearing blocked drains using safe and effective methods to maintain proper drainage and prevent backups.

Pipe Repair & Repiping

Repairing damaged pipes or replacing outdated piping systems to restore reliability and meet current plumbing standards.

Our Mission

At Plums Ten, we're dedicated to providing top-notch plumbing services to Manchester. With 10 years of experience, our team of licensed and certified plumbers is committed to delivering fast, reliable, and affordable solutions for all your plumbing needs. Our mission is to build long-term relationships with our customers, providing them with exceptional service and peace of mind.

Our Story

Founded in 1989, Plums Ten has grown from a small local business to a trusted name in the plumbing industry. Our founder, Kali Walker, started the company with a passion for providing excellent service and building long-term relationships with customers. Over the years, we've expanded our services to meet the growing needs of our community, but our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction remains the same.
